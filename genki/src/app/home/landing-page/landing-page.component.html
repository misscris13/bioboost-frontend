genki.

(n. /ɡˈɛŋki/)

genki is a japanese word that means the original state of living, or the spirit, mind, energy, and mood of a person. It is often used to describe someone who is in good spirits and young at heart.

microbiome.

the microbiome is the diverse community of microorganisms living in and on our bodies.
